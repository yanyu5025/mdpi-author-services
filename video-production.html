A guided production journey

From order to distribution

Your video is delivered within 15 working days of payment, excluding time spent waiting for feedback or revisions.

  1. 1

    Order

    Choose the video type you prefer and place your order. We will contact you shortly after payment is received.

  2. 2

    Script Writing

    Our team will create a script based on your research and submit it for your approval before production.

  3. 3

    Video Production

    The final video will be delivered within 15 working days of payment, excluding correspondence or revision delays.

  4. 4

    Revision

    After the draft video is produced, we will send it to you for feedback and make revisions until you are satisfied.

  5. 5

    Online

    After the revisions are finalized, we will upload the video to our platform and the journal page (if associated with MDPI).

  6. 6

    Dissemination

    We will distribute your video abstract across social media platforms, including Facebook, X, YouTube, and LinkedIn.
